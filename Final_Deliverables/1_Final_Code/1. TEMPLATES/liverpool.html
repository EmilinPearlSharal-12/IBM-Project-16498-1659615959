The University of Liverpool

About

The University of Liverpool was founded in 1881 to support students to become highly employable, creative, and culturally rich graduates. It is a public research university located in the city of Liverpool, UK. Currently, the university has over 22,000 students on campus with over 7,500 international student community. UL is among the top universities in the UK that offer students the best academic education. The United Kingdom is the home of many world-ranked universities and Liverpool is one of them. According to QS World University Ranking 2023, the University of Liverpool Ranking is 190 in the world and is one of the top public universities in Liverpool, United Kingdom. The Guardian has placed Liverpool among the top 50 universities in the UK. Currently, the university holds the 40th position in the country. The high ranking of the university makes it the most preferred choice to study in UK. The University of Liverpool Campus is located in Liverpool city. Occupying 100 acres, it contains 192 non-residential buildings that house 69 lecture theatres, 114 teaching areas, and research facilities. The University of Liverpool Courses are available in a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in popular disciplines including humanities and social sciences, science and engineering, health & life sciences, and management studies. Apart from this, it also offers a number of online programs as well. Since its establishment, the University of Liverpool has remained a global centre for advanced learning and innovation. Liverpool is a founding member of the Russell Group of universities and the N8 Group for research cooperation. 94% of its graduates are either employed or went to study within 6 months of graduation. If we check the University of Liverpool Accommodation then students get over 4,500 rooms both in Greenbank Student Village and at city campus residences. Students can enjoy their stay in Liverpool as they get fully furnished rooms, fresh bedding & towels, and a good range of kitchen equipment for you to enjoy the self-catering facilities. The University of Liverpool has a strong track record of offering scholarships to international students. The University of Liverpool Scholarships is available in a wide range of verities to support talented candidates from every background. The Liverpool Bursary, The Divock Origi Scholarship, Asylum Seekers Scholarship, Cowrie Foundation Scholarship, etc. are some of the popular scholarships program available at the university for students. If we talk about The University of Liverpool Notable Alumni, then the university has a network of over 219,000 in over 170 countries. Description

University of Liverpool offers on-campus accommodation to its students

Students have self-catering options as well as catered accommodation or can live at two Student villages in Liverpool's main student suburban area

The average rent is 500 to 700 Pounds/month

The residence type includes studio apartments, 3 bedroom apartments, 4 bedroom apartments and shared apartments

These apartments are available on a room by room basis and rent is inclusive of Gas, Water, Electricity, TV Licence and Internet.

There are a number of takeaways and shops close by and also within walking distance of famous sights across Liverpool
